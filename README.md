# Customer Behaviour Analysis & Dashboard
## Project Overview

This project analyzes customer purchasing behavior using sales data and presents insights through a Customer Behaviour Dashboard.

**The goal of this project is to**:

* Understand customer demographics

* Analyze purchasing patterns

* Compare subscription vs non-subscription behavior

* Identify top-performing products and categories

* Evaluate revenue contribution across segments

 ## Analytical Approach

1.**Data Cleaning & Preparation**:

* Removed inconsistencies and standardized categorical variables

* Engineered customer segments (New, Returning, Loyal)

* Created calculated metrics for revenue contribution and discount percentages

2.**SQL-Based Business Analysis**:

* Executed advanced aggregation queries including:

* Revenue by gender

* Discount-based high-spending customers

* Top-rated products

* Shipping type comparison

* Subscription spending comparison

* Revenue contribution by age group

* Repeat buyer subscription analysis

* Top products by category
  Dashboard Development (Power BI)

3.**The dashboard includes**:

* KPI Cards (Total Customers, Avg Spend, Avg Rating)

* Revenue by Category

* Sales Volume by Category

* Revenue by Age Group

* Subscription Distribution

* Customer Segment Breakdown

* Interactive Filters

The dashboard is designed for executive-level summary and drill-down analysis.


## Key Results & Insights

**Total Customers Analyzed**: 3,900

**Average Purchase Value**: $59.76

**Average Customer Rating**: 3.75

**Subscription Rate**: 27%

## Key Insights
 **Revenue by Gender**

* Male customers generated higher total revenue compared to female customers.

 **Customer Segments**
* Loyal customers: 3116

* Returning customers: 701

* New customers: 83

* Most revenue comes from loyal customers.

 **Revenue by Age Group**

* Middle-aged customers contribute the highest revenue.

 Followed by young adults, seniors, and adults.
 
 **Shipping Comparison**

* Express Shipping: Higher average purchase amount

* Standard Shipping: Slightly lower average

 **Top Performing Categories**
* Clothing generates the highest revenue.

* Accessories and Footwear follow.
